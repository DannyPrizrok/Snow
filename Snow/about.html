Our Story Founded in 2018, Snow embarked on a visionary journey, fueled by a passion for design excellence. From our inception, we set out to redefine spaces through a harmonious blend of innovation and meticulous craftsmanship. In 2020, our founders envisioned more than a furniture brand — they envisioned an immersive experience where each piece tells a story of dedication and timeless elegance. Our commitment to excellence has evolved over the years, making Snow synonymous with superior quality and design mastery. Fast forward to the present, where Snow stands as a testament to our journey. Every creation is a reflection of years of dedication, precision, and an unwavering commitment to delivering not just furniture but elevated lifestyles.
First Design In 2019, Snow introduced its inaugural design—an exceptional chair that quickly became a standout bestseller. This iconic creation not only marked our entry into the world of furniture but also established a benchmark for design excellence. Meticulously crafted for both aesthetics and comfort, the chair resonated with our clientele, reflecting our commitment to quality. Its widespread popularity wasn't just a testament to its visual appeal but also to the enduring principles of craftsmanship embedded in every detail. This chair remains an enduring symbol of Snow's dedication to creating timeless, beloved pieces that transcend trends and captivate discerning tastes.
Our Office In 2021, we unveiled a cutting-edge modern office, an embodiment of our commitment to innovation and contemporary workspaces. Nestled in Istanbul, this vibrant hub seamlessly merges style with functionality. From sleek workstations to collaborative zones, every aspect is meticulously designed to foster creativity and productivity. The space reflects our dedication to a dynamic work environment, where ideas flourish and collaboration thrives. Our modern office isn't just a physical space; it's a testament to our vision for the future of work. Step into the pulse of design excellence at Snow's 2021-born workspace—a realm where the present meets limitless possibilities.
Core Team Since our inception, Snow has been guided by a steadfast core team, pioneers integral to our journey from the very start. This cohesive unit, assembled with a diverse range of talents, has fueled our growth and shaped our identity. Their shared dedication to design excellence and innovation has been the bedrock of our success. As architects of our vision, this original team embodies the spirit and resilience that define Snow. Their unwavering commitment has not only weathered challenges but propelled us to new heights, making them the driving force behind our narrative of inspired living and ongoing success. They remain the heartbeat of Snow's enduring impact in design.
